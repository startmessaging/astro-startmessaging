## Commission Structure

StartMessaging uses a per-message commission model. Every OTP or SMS
        sent by a user you referred earns you a fixed commission amount. The
        commission is calculated on each individual message, so your earnings
        scale linearly with the volume your referrals send.


Key details about the commission structure:



- **Per-message rate:** The commission rate is set by the admin-managed commission settings and is visible in your referral dashboard. Rates are competitive within the industry.
- **No cap on earnings:** There is no maximum limit on how much you can earn per month or in total. Your income is directly proportional to the volume your referrals generate.
- **Transparent tracking:** Every commission event is logged with the message details, amount earned, and status. You can review your complete commission history in the dashboard.
- **Status flow:** Commissions start as "pending," move to "earned" once confirmed, and finally to "paid" after the monthly payout is processed.



To illustrate the potential: if you refer 10 users who each send 1,000
        OTPs per month, you earn commissions on 10,000 messages every single
        month. As those businesses grow and their OTP volumes increase, your
        earnings grow automatically without any additional effort on your part.


## Threshold and Payouts

The payout process is designed to be simple and predictable:



- **Minimum threshold:** Commissions accumulate until they reach the minimum payout threshold. This threshold is set to ensure efficient payment processing and is visible in your dashboard.
- **Monthly payout cycle:** Payouts are processed on a monthly basis. Once your accumulated earnings cross the threshold, the payout is included in the next monthly cycle.
- **Payout status tracking:** Every payout has a status that you can track: pending, processing, paid, or failed. If a payout fails (e.g., due to incorrect bank details), you will be notified and the amount remains in your account for the next cycle.
- **Commission aggregation:** Monthly payouts aggregate all individual commissions earned during the period. You receive one consolidated payment rather than per-message transfers.



The threshold-based system means smaller commissions accumulate over
        time. Even if individual referrals send modest volumes initially, the
        combined earnings across all your referrals add up. Patience pays off in
        this model.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

We have seen referrers make several avoidable mistakes that limit their
        earnings:



- **Spamming communities:** Posting your referral code everywhere without context gets you banned and hurts your reputation. Always provide genuine value alongside your recommendation.
- **Ignoring follow-up:** Many referred users sign up but do not complete their integration. A simple follow-up message asking if they need help getting started can significantly improve conversion from registered to qualified status.
- **Targeting the wrong audience:** Referring users who have no need for SMS services wastes everyone's time. Focus on developers and businesses actively building products that require phone verification.
- **Not tracking performance:** Without checking your dashboard regularly, you cannot know which strategies are working. Review your stats monthly and double down on what generates results.
- **Overpromising:** Be honest about what StartMessaging offers. Inflating claims about delivery rates, pricing, or features leads to disappointed referrals who churn quickly, cutting off your commission stream.
